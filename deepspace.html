Atmospheric Study No. 1042

The Description

A study of high-altitude light scattering during the blue hour. At this elevation, the air is thin enough to reveal the subtle gradients of the ozone layer.

History

Captured in the Swiss Alps, December 2025. This frame represents the intersection of geographic permanence and the fleeting nature of dawn.
